HCD Learning's ChangeMan simulation game helps OCLA to build organizational skills

August 15, 2014

25 Participants from OCLA has participated ChangeMan simulation which received a warm response. Christian Harpelund, HCD's behavioral expert from Denmark, introduced the concepts of probability of failure, reasons for the change and change cycle. With wide range of Organizational context, students gained exposure to theory and hands-on experience in change management, along with having fun. After introducing the concepts, the participants played the "ChangeMan" simulation game and made series of decisions. This form of decisions making simulates the real-life change situations

Once the decisions were made, the results were interpreted by Christian, and explained the decisions in context of theoretical frameworks. This was followed by role-play by students, which fun and an interesting learning experience for the participants. Having familiarized themselves with process and software, they repeated the decision making exercise for three more rounds to consolidate their learnings and apply them. Change Management is not just about change, it is an art related to communication and understanding and the whole session reinforced this information firmly.
